Language

Before embarking on this lesson on language it is important that I clear up some vocabulary that I may use throughout this discussion. Because I have spent most of my educational career studying languages as a passion, Spanish in particular, I have a tendency to refer to the nationally spoken Spanish language as Castellano or Castilian. This word refers to the region of Castilla y Leon and Castilla de la Mancha that modernized the Spanish country under one central ruler.

Also, although it may be first nature to come and view these pages first given that my project is called the Dialect Effect, I would suggest that you read through the Modern Politics and History sections. If you decide not to, you may see small reminders on pages where that information may be particularly relevant.
